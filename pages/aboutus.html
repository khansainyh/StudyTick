Welcome to StudyTick

StudyTick, a brainchild of four passionate college students from Universitas Negeri Semarang, was born as a part of a college project. What started as an academic endeavor has now evolved into a powerful tool designed to transform the way students approach productivity.

Our Mission

At StudyTick, our mission is clear and focused: to enhance the productivity of students. We understand the challenges that students face in managing their time and staying focused on their academic pursuits. StudyTick is our solution to these challenges, providing a user-friendly Pomodoro timer that empowers students to make the most out of their study sessions.

Our Values

StudyTick is driven by the belief that students can achieve more when equipped with the right tools. Our application is designed to help students improve their productivity through focused study sessions and strategic breaks. We believe that a balanced approach to studying leads to better academic performance and overall well-being.

Target Market

StudyTick is tailored for middle high school, high school, and college students. We understand the unique demands of academic life at these levels, and our application is crafted to address the specific needs of students in these stages of their education journey.
